Diagnostic Services

Regular check-ups, cleanings, and treatments like fluoride application to prevent dental problems. Using tools like X-rays to identify issues like cavities, gum disease, and other conditions.

Dental clinic

A dental clinic is a healthcare facility specializing in oral health, providing services like preventative care, diagnostics, and treatment of dental issues. They offer a range of procedures from routine check-ups and cleanings to restorative work like fillings and root canals, and even cosmetic dentistry and orthodontics. Dental clinics play a crucial role in maintaining and improving patients' oral health through various services and procedures.

Orthodontics

Treatments like braces to correct misaligned teeth and improve bite. Procedures like extractions, dental implants, and other surgical interventions. Providing guidance on proper oral hygiene practices.
